Club Rules

This page is for the rules of the club, which are to be followed:

List of Rules:

  1. Members should suggest and vote on book titles for the club to read.
  2. Members should be respectful of others' opinions and be open to discussing different perspectives on the books.
  3. Members should set aside time for meetings to discuss the books and other book-related topics.
  4. The club should have book exchanges or book swaps.
  5. The club should have an online forum or group chat to discuss books and book recommendations outside of the in-person meetings.
  6. Members should be open to new book genres and give them a chance.
  7. Members should share their thoughts and opinions about the books they read.
  8. Members should have a friendly and welcoming attitude towards new members.
