About Book Lover's Club

This club is for book lovers to share their joy of reading with their fellow schoolmates. This is a group of people who have a shared love for reading and the written word. They come together to discuss books, share book recommendations, and engage in book related activities such as book clubs, book swaps, and book donations. The primary aim of a book lovers' club is to foster a love for literature, to broaden one's reading horizons and to create a community of like-minded people who appreciate the written word. The club should embody Benedictine values such as Love of Christ and Neighbor. A book lovers' club that adheres to these values would prioritize kindness, compassion, and charity in its activities, making it a welcoming community for all book lovers, regardless of background, and a place where true community can be found.
